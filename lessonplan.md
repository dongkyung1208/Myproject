### 🏫 Learner Profile & Class Setting
* **Target Level:** 1st-year high school students in a mixed-proficiency classroom (25–27 students in total).
  * *Low-level learners (approx. 5 students):* Lack foundational knowledge of basic English vocabulary and sentence structures.
  * *High-level learners (approx. 5 students):* Capable of completing assigned tasks independently without the teacher's assistance.
  * *Intermediate-level learners (the remaining majority):* Possess a general understanding but frequently make minor mistakes.
    
* **Class Setting:** A fully connected 1:1 digital environment where all students can utilize their personal smartphones or tablets.
  
---

### 🎯 Lesson Topic & Learning Objectives
* **Lesson Topic:** Post-modification by Past Participles
* **Learning Objectives:**
  * Students will be able to review and identify correct past participle forms.
  * Students will be able to construct complete sentences using past participles to modify nouns from behind.
* **Target Language:** Past Participles as Post-modifiers (Structure: Noun + Past Participle Phrase)

### 🚶 Teaching Procedure

| Stage (Time) | Content / Focus | Student Activities | Teacher Role |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **1. Introduction**<br>

<br>(6 mins) | **Target Grammar Introduction**<br>

<br>Introducing the target grammar through textbook examples. | **Locate & Analyze:**<br>

<br>Find and analyze the highlighted sentences containing the target grammar in their reading worksheets. | **Elicit & Prompt:**<br>

<br>Present sentences from the main text containing the target grammar and prompt students to identify the core grammatical rule. |
| **2. Development 1**<br>

<br>(7 mins) | **Concept Explanation**<br>

<br>Grammar rules for post-modification by past participles. | **Listen & Fill in:**<br>

<br>Listen to the teacher's explanation and fill in the blanks on their grammar worksheets. | **Explain & Guide:**<br>

<br>Explain the concept of past participles and how they function to modify nouns from behind. |
| **3. Development 2**<br>

<br>(7 mins) | **Vocabulary Review**<br>

<br>Self-directed review of past participle forms. | **Self-Paced Learning:**<br>

<br>Access the "Voca Learning" tab on the app and review confusing past participle forms from Step 1 to Step 3. | **Monitor & Support:**<br>

<br>Guide students to the app and monitor their self-paced vocabulary review, ensuring everyone is on task. |
| **4. Development 3**<br>

<br>(20 mins) | **Differentiated Application**<br>

<br>Level-appropriate sentence construction practice. | **Differentiated Practice:**<br>

<br>Access the "Writing Practice" tab and complete tasks corresponding to their proficiency: Level 1 (Low), Level 2 (Intermediate), or Level 3 (High). | **Facilitate & Check:**<br>

<br>Monitor the classroom to ensure students are working on the appropriate level tab and provide individualized support if needed. |
| **5. Conclusion**<br>

<br>(10 mins) | **Wrap-up & Assignment**<br>

<br>File submission guide and next lesson preview. | **Download & Submit:**<br>

<br>Download their incorrect answers as an HWP file and submit them individually via the 'Riroschool' app. | **Instruct & Preview:**<br>

<br>Instruct students on the HWP file download and Riroschool submission process, and briefly preview the second core grammar point for the next class. |
